How it works

Level controls usable material tiers. Limit is the current cap. Target is the cap you want to reach. The planner considers tiers at or below your highest entered stat level, unless Exact tier is selected. Shopping List is the smallest material count found under the max-per-material cap. Final Limits show expected caps after feeding. The estimate uses average Limit breakpoints and assumes listed materials are fed from lower total gain to higher total gain.

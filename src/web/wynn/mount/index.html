How it works

Level controls usable material tiers. Limit is the current cap. Target is the cap you want to reach. If you train first, update Level manually. The selected tier is the highest known material tier that does not exceed the highest entered Level. Shopping List is the smallest material count found under the cap. Final Limits are capped at Target; extra gain is shown as unused overflow. The estimate uses average Limit breakpoints and assumes listed materials are fed from lower total gain to higher total gain.
